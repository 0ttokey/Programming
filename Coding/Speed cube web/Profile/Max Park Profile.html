Profile


Max Park

Max Park (born November 28, 2001) is a Rubik's Cube speedsolver who is the world record holder for the 3x3x3 single solve at 3.13 seconds
and is currently tied 2nd place for the world record average of five 3x3x3 solves at 4.86 seconds, set on September 24, 2022.
Park first held this record from April 23, 2017 to June 28, 2017 and was the only cuber other than Feliks Zemdegs to hold the record between
September 27, 2009 and June 5, 2021.Park has also set multiple world records in speedsolving in the 4x4x4, 5x5x5, 6x6x6, and 7x7x7 cubes as well as the 3x3x3 One-Handed event.As of January 28, 2023, he has won 401 total events in World Cube Association competitions.
